On December 1 2018, SPAN (www.spanhealth.com) took the first step towards making diagnostics a key vertical. The laboratories are located in Mumbai, Pune, Barshi, and Aurangabad. The management is committed to ensuring that the quality of our services continues to meet and exceed your requirements while expanding the number of tests performed locally and also improving the TAT significantly. Diagnostica SPAN has plans of expanding its operations pan-India through organic and franchisee models designed to provide a robust and reliable one-stop solution. The Company’s broad spectrum of services offer around 1200 clinical laboratory tests and various profiles. Various test combinations, specific to a disease or disorder and wellness profiles used for health and fitness screening, form a part of the Company’s service profile. One can choose and book blood tests for: CBC, Blood sugar, Thyroid, Allergy, Pregnancy, Heart profile, Diabetes profile, Liver profile, Vitamin B12 or Vitamin D3 and many more.

Why Choose Us

15 years of experience

Celebrating 15 years of Responsibility, Commitment & Trust: Since 2005, when SPAN began, our mission has always been to serve the community by partnering with care-givers and bringing home leading edge technologies that advance the standard of medical practice in India.

Vein to Vein Solution

Our extensive product range and capabilities enable us to focus on providing a complete set of solutions today, facilitating the safe and effective passage of blood from the Donor’s vein to the Recipient’s vein. Today, SPAN is the only organization serving the transfusion services community with a comprehensive end-to-end range of industry-leading blood screening solutions for Transfusion Transmitted Infections (TTI) and Immuno-Hematology (IH) applications.
