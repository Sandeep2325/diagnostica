About Span

On December 1 2018, SPAN (www.spanhealth.com) took the first step towards making diagnostics a key vertical. The laboratories are located in Mumbai, Pune, Barshi, and Aurangabad.

The management is committed to ensuring that the quality of our services continues to meet and exceed your requirements while expanding the number of tests performed locally and also improving the TAT significantly. Diagnostica SPAN has plans of expanding its operations pan-India through organic and franchisee models designed to provide a robust and reliable one-stop solution.
